About Daura Youth Camp

The Daura Youth Camp is a 3-day gathering of passionate and vibrant young people designed to inspire, equip, and connect the next generation of leaders. Holding from Friday to Sunday in the historic city of Daura, the camp brings together youths for a time of learning, spiritual upliftment, personal growth, and fun. Our mission is to foster a community where young minds can thrive, share ideas, and build lasting friendships while being guided by positive values and principles.
